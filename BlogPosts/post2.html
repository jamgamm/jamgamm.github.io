Summer Throwback

July 7, 2018


A few summers ago my family and I took a day trip to Montana. Actually, the reason for this was because we were craving Kripsy Kreme dounuts. The closest Kripsy Kreme to Calgary is in Kalispell, Montana so we decided to take a quick trip over the border!

On the way there we also took a trip through Glacier National Park. It's a beautiful park with lots of nature and mountain ranges. This picture of a lake and mountains is still one of my favourite summer pictures I've taken to this day.
