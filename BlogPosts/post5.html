Trip to Vancouver

Aug 28, 2022


I recently went on a trip to Vancouver this summer with my friends. It was a lot of fun!

We explored downtown Vancouver and also went around Burnaby and Richmond. Personally, I like Richmond the most, mainly because there's so much great food to eat there! Something I like to do when I visit in the summer is go to the night market and buy a bunch of different things to eat. A few of my favourite things to get there are grilled squid, meat skewers, and dango.

We also went to a hotpot place in Richmond for dinner one night. It was my friend's first time having hotpot and I'm glad they enjoyed it. The broths we got were curry and tomato, and we got a variety of meats including beef, lamb, and tripe. The picture is actually our first round, we ordered more meat after it!

I had a great time and am looking forward to the next time I can go back!
