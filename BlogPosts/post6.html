Wind Turbines are BIG

Jun 22, 2023


During my co-op term I was lucky enough to do a site visit to a wind farm and solar farm that our company monitors. I was even allowed to climb a wind turbine!

Honestly, it was pretty tough to climb, and going up a 60 meter tower was scarier than I thought. But still super cool! The view from the top was amazing, and seeing all the parts inside the nacelle was really interesting too.

It really helped put into perspective the inner workings of the turbines parts I read about as part of my role in my co-op. Seeing both wind turbines and solar panels in person gave me a better understanding of what I deal with in my work life and showed how it has a real life effect!
