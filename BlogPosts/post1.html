Going out for Coffee

April 29, 2022


I went out with a couple of friends to check out And Some Cafe. It's a little coffee shop located in Kengsington. They offer a variety of coffees, lemonades, and snacks like muffins and cakes. I got the strawberry latte and it was very good.

There's also a small flower shop at the front of the cafe which is really cute! My friends and I picked out some flowers for our own bouquets. But if you're unsure of what flowers to choose, you can also ask the florist to make a bouquet based on a colour theme, which I think is a nice option.

Overall it was a fun time! I definitely recommend this place for a nice spot to chill out.
