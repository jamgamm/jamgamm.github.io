Another Cafe Post

Sep 2, 2023


Located in Chinatown is a place called Cafe Ambient. It's a small place located on a second floor that's a cafe during the daytime and turns into a cocktail bar in the evening.

I've gone here several times during different times of day, and it's a great spot to sit down to chill with some friends. In the afternoon you can grab a coffee and a croffle, or in the evening you can dine in for some finger foods, like Korean fried chicken, fries, or tacos, and also drink a Soju cocktail!

Their dinner service starts around 6pm and they're open until midnight on most days. I totally recommend this spot for small groups to do some late night hang outs in downtown's Chinatown!
